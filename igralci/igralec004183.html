Career Notes
made his debut with TVG Baskets Trier (Germany), Regionalliga, during the 2007-08 season, also making his debut with TBB Trier, Bundesliga...played with TBB Trier and DJK MJC Trier, Regionalliga during the 2008-09 season...played with TBB Trier and Saar-Pfalz Braves, Pro-A, during the 2009-10 season...played with TBB Trier till the 2011-12 championship...signed for the 2012-13 season by Brose Baskets Bamberg...played there also the 2013-14 championship...moved to Serbia for the 2014-15 season, signed by Crvena Zvezda Belgrade...played there also the 2015-16 championship...moved to Israel for the 2016-17 season, signed by Maccabi Tel Aviv...in January'17 moved to Germany, signed by FC Bayern Munich...he's still playing there.
Miscellaneous
named the 2015-16 EuroLeague Round 8 MVP...won the 2012-13 German National Championship with Brose Baskets Bamberg...won the 2014-15 and 2015-16 Serbian National Championship with Crvena Zvezda Belgrade…won the 2015 Serbian National Cup with Crvena Zvezda Belgrade…won the 2013 German Supercup with Brose Baskets Bamberg...won the 2015 and 2016 Adriatic League with Crvena Zvezda Belgrade…played the 2012, 2013 and 2014 German All Star Game...has been member of the German U-18, U-20 and University National Team...played at the 2008 European U-18 Championship...played at the 2009 World University Games...played at the 2010 European U-20 Championship...member of the German National Team...played at the 2013 European Championship.
