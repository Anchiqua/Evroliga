Career Notes
grew up with Apoel Nicosia (Cyprus) youth teams...moved to Greece for the 2009-10 season, signed by Aris Thessaloniki, played with the junior team...made his debut with the main team during the 2011-12 season...played there till the 2014-15 championship...moved to Spain for the 2015-16 season, signed by FC Barcelona...he's still playing there.
Miscellaneous
member of the Bulgarian National Team...has been member of the won the 2015 Spanish Super Cup with FC BarcelonaBulgarian U-16, U-18 and U-20 National Team...played at the 2010 and 2011 European U-16 Championships...played at the 2012 and 2013 European U-18 Championships...played at the 2012 and 2014 European U-20 Championships...led the 2011 European U-16 Championship in scoring (27.1 ppg.)...led the 2014 European U-20 Championship in scoring (19.3 ppg.) and rebounding (11.2 rpg.).
