Career Notes
grew up with KK Zagreb (Croatia) juniorsmade his debut with KK Zagreb during the 2002-03 seasonplayed there till the 2011-12 championship...moved to Spain for the 2012-13 season, signed by CB Malaga..moved to Russia for the 2013-14 season, signed by Lokomotiv Kuban...played there also the 2014-15 championship...moved to Italy for the 2015-16 season, signed by Olimpia Milano...played there also the 2016-17 championship...moved to Turkey for the 2017-18 season, signed by Anadolu Efes Istanbul.
Miscellaneous
