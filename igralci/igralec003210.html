Career Notes
grew up with Zalgiris Kaunas (Lithuania) juniors…made his debut with Zalgiris 2, NKL, during the 2012-13 season…played there also the 2013-14 championship…signed for the 2014-15 season by BC Zalgiris Kaunas...played there till the 2016-17 championship...moved to Greece for the 2017-18 season, signed by Panathinaikos Athens.
Miscellaneous
won the 2014-15, 2015-16 and 2016-17 Lithuanian National Championship with BC Zalgiris Kaunas...won the 2015 and 2017 Lithuanian National Cup with Zalgiris Kaunas…member of the Lithuanian National Team...won the silver medal at the 2015 European Championship…has been member of the U-16, U-18, U-19 and U-20 National Team…won the silver medal at the 2010 European U-16 Championship…won the silver medal at the 2012 European U-18 Championship…won the bronze medal at the 2013 World U-19 Championship…played at the 2011 European U-18 Championship…played at the 2014 European U-20 Championship.
