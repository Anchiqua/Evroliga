Career Notes
made his debut with ASC Theresianum Mainz (Germany), Regionalliga, during the 2007-08 season…played high-school basketball at Cheyenne Mountain (2008-09)…signed for the 2009-10 season by ASC Theresianum Mainz…signed for the 2010-11 season by TV 1862 Langen, ProB…played college basketball at Boston College (2011-15)…back to Germany for the 2015-16 season, signed by Brose Bamberg...he's still playing there.
Miscellaneous
won the 2015-16 and 2016-17 German National Championship with Brose Bamberg…won the 2017 German National Cup with Brose Bamberg…played the 2016 and 2017 German All Star Game...has been member of the German U-16, U-18 and U-20 National Team…played at the 2009 European U-16 ‘B' Championship…played at the 2010 European U-18 Championship…played at the 2011 and 2012 European U-20 Championships…member of the German National Team...played at the 2017 European Championship.
