Career Notes
grew up with La Salle Maò and CB i Uniò Manresana (Spain) youth teams...made his debut with Basquet Manresa during the 2005-06 season, also playing with Finques Olesa, in the EBA...played with Basquet Manresa also the 2006-07 championship...on May'07 signed by Real Madrid CF...he's still playing there.
Miscellaneous
won the 2015 EuroLeague with Real Madrid CF…won the 2015 Intercontinental Cup with Real Madrid CF…named the 2016-17 EuroLeague MVP...named to the 2016-17 All-EuroLeague First Team...named to the 2010-11 All-EuroLeague Second Team...named 2012-13 EuroLeague April MVP...named 2016-17 EuroLeague November MVP...named 2016-17 EuroLeague Round 7 and 27 MVP...member of the Spanish National Team…won the gold medal at the 2009, 2011 and 2015 European Championships...won the silver medal at the 2012 Olympic Games...won the bronze medal at the 2013 European Championship...won the bronze medal at the 2016 Olympic Games…played at the 2010 and 2014 World Championships...won the 2012-13, 2014-15 and 2015-16 Spanish National Championship with Real Madrid CF...won the 2012, 2014, 2015, 2016 and 2017 Spanish National Cup with Real Madrid CF...won the 2012, 2013 and 2014 Spanish Super Cup with Real Madrid CF...named the 2012 Spanish National Cup Final MVP...has been member of the Spanish U-18 and U-20 National Team...won the gold medal at the 2004 European U-18 Championship...played at the 2005 European U-18 Championship...won the silver medal at the 2007 European U-20 Championship...played at the 2006 European U-20 Championship.
