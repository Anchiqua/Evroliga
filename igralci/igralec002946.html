Career Notes
played college basketball at Charleston (2003-07)...moved to Australia for the 2007-08 season, signed by Sydney Kings...moved to France for the 2008-09 season, signed by Hyeres-Toulon...on December'08 moved to Belgium, signed by BC Oostende...moved to Italy for the 2009-10 season, signed by Prima Veroli, Legadue...moved to Croatia for the 2010-11 season, signed by Cedevita Zagreb...played there also the 2011-12 championship...moved to Spain for the 2012-13 season, signed by Real Madrid CF...played there also the 2013-14 championship...moved to Turkey for the 2014-15 season, signed by Anadolu Efes Istanbul...moved to Russia for the 2015-16 season, signed by Lokomotiv Kuban Krasnodar...back to Spain for the 2016-17 season, signed by Real Madrid CF.
Miscellaneous
named the 2010-11 EuroCup MVP...named to the 2010-11 EuroCup All-First Team...named the 2010-11 EuroCup Week-6 MVP...won the 2012-13 Spanish National Championship with Real Madrid CF...won the 2012 Croatian National Cup with Cedevita Zagreb...won the 2014 and 2017 Spanish National Cup with Real Madrid...won the 2015 Turkish National Cup with Anadolu Efes Istanbul…won the 2012 and 2013 Spanish Super Cup with Real Madrid CF...played the 2012 Croatian All Star Game...named to the 2006-07 All-Southern Conference 1st Team...member of the Croatian National Team...played at the 2011, 2013 and 2015 European Championships.
