Career Notes
played college basketball at Fordham (2004-08)...moved to Korea for the 2008-09 season, signed by Mobis Phoebus...played there also the 2009-10 championship...moved to Greece for the 2010-11 season, signed by Aris Thessaloniki...on February'11 moved to Israel, signed by Bnei Hasharon...signed for the 2011-12 season by Hapoel Holon...moved to Italy for the 2012-13 season, signed by Pallacanestro Varese...moved to Greece for the 2013-14 season, signed by Olympiacos Piraeus...played there also the 2014-15 championship...moved to Turkey for the 2015-16 season, signed by Anadolu Efes Istanbul...he's still playing there.
Miscellaneous
2013-14 and 2014-15 EuroLeague Best Defender...named to the 2016-17 All-EuroLeague Second Team...named the 2013-14 EuroLeague Round 3 and Playoffs Game 3 MVP...named the 2016-17 EuroLeague Playoffs Game 3 MVP...led the 2013-14 EuroLeague in blocks (1.31 bpg.)...won the 2013 Intercontinental Cup with Olympiacos Piraeus…won the 2010 Korean League with Mobis Phoebus...won the 2014-15 Greek National Championship with Olympiacos Piraeus...played the 2012 Israeli All Star Game...played the 2013 Italian All Star Game...played the 2017 Turkish All Star Game...led the 2009-10 Korean League in blocked shots (2.3 bspg.)...led the 2012-13 Italian League in blocked shots (2.1 bspg.)...named to the 2005-06 All-Atlantic 10 1st Team...named to the 2004-05, 2005-06 and 2007-08 All-Atlantic 10 2nd Team.
