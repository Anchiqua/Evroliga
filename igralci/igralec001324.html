Career Notes
played college basketball at Troy State (2004-06)...moved to France for the 2006-07 season, signed by Saint-Etienne, Pro-B...in May'07, signed by BCM Gravelines-Dunkerque...moved to Poland for the 2007-08 season, signed by Polpak Swiecie...moved to Italy for the 2008-09 season, signed by Benetton Basket Treviso…in February'09 moved to France, signed by MSB Le Mans…signed for the 2009-10 season by Asvel Lyon Villeurbanne...in March'10 moved to Italy, signed by Benetton Basket Treviso...signed for the 2010-11 season by Pallacanestro Brindisi...back to France for the 2011-12 season, signed by JDA DIjon...moved to Turkey for the 2012-13 season, signed by Pinar Karsiyaka...played there till the 2014-15 championship...signed for the 2015-16 season by Fenerbahce Istanbul...he's still playing there.
Miscellaneous
won the 2017 EuroLeague with Fenerbahce Istanbul…named the 2014-15 EuroCup Second Team...won the 2014-15 Turkish National Championship with Pinar Karsiyaka Izmir…won the 2015-16 and 2016-17 Turkish National Championship with Fenerbahce Istanbul…won the 2009 Semain d'As with MSB Le Mans…won the 2009 French National Cup with MSB Le Mans…won the 2014 Turkish National Cup with Pinar Karsiyaka Izmir…won the 2016 Turkish National Cup with Fenerbahce Istanbul…won the 2016 President Cup with Fenerbahce Istanbul…named 2016 President Cup MVP...played the 2013, 2015 and 2016 Turkish All Star Game...led the 2007-08 Polish League in assists (5.1 apg.)...named to the 2005-06 All-Sun Belt 1st Team...member of the Turkish National Team...played at the 2015 European Championship.
