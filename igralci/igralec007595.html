Career Notes
grew up with Malvin Montevideo (Uruguay) juniors…made his debut with Malvin during the 2006-07 season…played there till the 2013-14 championship…moved to Argentina for the 2014-15 season, signed by Obras Sanitarias Buenos Aires…played there also the 2015-16 championship…moved to Italy for the 2016-17 season, signed by Capo d'Orlando Basket…in December'16 moved to Turkey, signed by Galatasaray Istanbul.
Miscellaneous
won the 2010-11 and 2013-14 Uruguayan National Championship with Malvin Montevideo…member of the Uruguayan National Team…won the bronze medal at the 2010, 2012, 2014 and 2016 South American Championships…played at the 2011, 2013 and 2015 Americas Championships…played at the 2011 Panamerican Games…has been member of the Uruguayan U-16, U-17 and U-18 National Team…played at the 2008 South American U-17 Championship…played at the 2008 Americas U-18 Championship.
