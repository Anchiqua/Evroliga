Career Notes
played college basketball at UTPA (2006-08)...moved to Mexico for the 2008-09 season, signed by Algodoneros de la Comarca...signed for the 2009-10 season by Halcones Xalapa...in October'09 signed by Halcones Cordoba....signed for the 2010-11 season by Algodoneros de la Comarca...in December'10 signed by Halcones Rojos de Veracruz...played there also the 2011-12 season, playing during the 2011 and 2012 Summer in Venezuela with Gigantes de Guayana...moved to Israel for the 2012-13 season, signed by Maccabi Haifa...moved to Turkey for the 2013-14 season, signed by Trabzonspor Basketball...moved to Venezuela in April'14, signed by Gigantes de Guayana...moved to Mexico for the 2014-15 season, signed by Halcones Rojos de Veracruz...in November'15, moved to Russia, signed by Avtodor Saratov...in November'16 signed by Unics Kazan.
Miscellaneous
named the 2015-16 Eurocup Round 7 MVP...won the 2011-12 Mexican LNBP with Halcones Rojos de Veracruz...won the 2012-13 Israeli National Championship with Maccabi Haifa...played the 2012 and 2015 Mexican All Star Game...member of the Mexican National Team...won the gold medal at the 2013 Americas Championship...won the gold medal at the 2014 Centrobasket...won the silver medal at the 2011 Panamerican Games...won the silver medal at the 2016 Centrobasket...played at the 2010 and 2012 Centrobasket...played at the 2015 Americas Championship...played at the 2014 World Championship.
