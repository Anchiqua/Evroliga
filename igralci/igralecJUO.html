Career Notes
grew up with Keravnos Larissa (Greece) youth teams…made his debut with Larissa, A2, during the 1999-00 season…played there also the 2000-01 championship…signed for the 2001-02 season by Maroussi Athens…played there till the 2004-05 championship…signed for the 2005-06 season by Panathinaikos Athens...signed for the 2006-07 season by Houston Rockets...back to Greece for the 2007-08 season, signed by Panathinaikos Athens...played there till the 2009-10 championship...signed for the 2010-11 season by Olympiacos Piraeus...he's still playing there.
Miscellaneous
won the 2009 EuroLeague with Panathinaikos Athens...won the 2012 and 2013 EuroLeague with Olympiacos Piraues...named the 2009, 2012 and 2013 EuroLeague Final Four MVP...named the 2012-13 EuroLeague MVP...named to the 2011-12, 2012-13 and 2014-15 All-EuroLeague First Team...named to the 2005-06, 2008-09, 2010-11 and 2013-14 All-EuroLeague 2nd Team…named the 2011-12 EuroLeague February MVP...named the 2012-13 EuroLeague November MVP...named the 2014-15 EuroLeague October MVP...named the 2005-06 EuroLeague Week-2 MVP...named the 2013-14 EuroLeague Round 8 and Top 16 Round 1 MVP...named the 2016-17 EuroLeague Round 2 and Playoffs Game 5 MVP...led the 2011-12, 2012-13 and 2014-15 EuroLeague in free throws made (110, 122, 104)…led the 2012-13 EuroLeague in assists (170)...won the 2013 Intercontinental Cup with Olympiacos Piraeus…member of the Greek National Team…won the gold medal at the 2005 European Championship...won the silver medal at the 2006 World Championship...played at the 2004 and 2008 Olympic Games...played at the 2007, 2013 and 2015 European Championships...played at the 2010 World Championship...won the 2005-06, 2007-08, 2008-09 and 2009-10 Greek National Championship with Panathinaikos Athens…won the 2011-12, 2014-15 and 2015-16 Greek National Championship with Olympiacos Piraeus...won the 2006, 2008 and 2009 Greek National Cup with Panathinaikos Athens…won the 2011 Greek National Cup with Olympiacos Piraeus...named the 2011 Greek Cup Final MVP...played the 2005, 2008, 2009, 2010 and 2011 Greek All Star Game…has been member of the Greek U-18 and U-20 National Team…won the bronze medal at the 2000 European U-18 Championship…won the gold medal at the 2002 European U-20 Championship.
